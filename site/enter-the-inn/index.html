A Word from the Keeper

Enter the Inn

I didn't set out to write about an inn. I set out to write about a woman who was tired of fighting. About what happens after the quest, after the battle, after everyone goes home and discovers they don't quite fit anymore.

She found an inn at a crossroads. Five roads meeting. Five kinds of traveler. And a building that seemed to know things — which room to give you, how you took your tea, whether you needed company or quiet.

I write from a drafty office with too many houseplants and a mug that's never quite empty. The Crossroads Inn is a six-book series — cozy fantasy with a slow-burn romance streak, found family, soft magic, and the kind of warmth you feel in your chest when you finally sit down after a long walk.

If you've ever wanted a book that feels like pulling a chair up to a fire in an inn where nobody expects anything from you — you've found the right place.

— Jason

The Crossroads Inn

The inn at the crossroads doesn't choose its keeper. It keeps her.

An ex-mercenary trades her sword for a soup ladle and inherits an inn that sits at the meeting of five roads. The rooms rearrange themselves. The regulars aren't entirely human. And the door opens for anyone the inn decides needs saving — whether they asked for it or not.

A novel of warmth, mystery, and the roads that lead us home.
